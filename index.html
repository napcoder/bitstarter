So, you know a lot of agile, you're the coolest PO in your company and you have such wonderful ideas to improve productivity of your team. Do you want to make all these concepts tangible?

The first things you will do are post-its. A lot of post-its all around your meeting room. Really, tons of post-its. Then you realize, we are in the age of internet, web and smartphones, and you look at the mirror: you, alone, with your blackboard and your post-its, whilst all the other cool guy are laughing at your back.

So you choose to change. What's the coolest geek things all the agile team is talking about? It's Trello baby! And you start to Trellify yourself, discovering a world of virtual boards and post-its you never heard before. What a beautiful thing! You create a lot of cards, and a lot of boards too, and start to move cards: from todo, to done and back to doing, and you do this for about 2 weeks. The end of the sprint is near, you stop to play and start to do something useful as PO. And then you realize: "Great Jupiter, I have no data to analyze!"

It's sad. Trello is beautiful, but here we are rude men, we make scrum, not post-its!
What you need is a tool that knows what means the word "sprint".
What you need is a tool that draw your burndown charts
What you need is a tool that help you to know clearly what's happening in every moment of the sprint
What you need is a tool that can divide a bord with cool horizontal strips

What you really need is Scrello!

FAQ

What is Scrello?
Scrello is a plugin to optimize the user experience of Trello for people using a SCRUM agile methodology
Does the "Scre" part of the name stay for "Scream"?
Eew, no. It stays for Scrum. But of course, if you want you can think it stays for Scream. And then you can scream. You can scream a lot.
There was really the need of yet another plugin for Trello?
Of course yes, just ask your Product Owner.
